Les dates les plus marquantes dans la vie de Stephen Hawking

09 January 1942

Hawking Writes His Thesis

Stephen Hawking was born in Oxford, England. His birth was exactly 300 years after the death of Galileo.

02 April 1958

Years at St. Albans School

Stephen Hawking attended St. Albans School in North London from 1953 to 1958. It was while he was at this school that he developed a passion for mathematics.

02 October 1959

Hawking Attends Oxford University

At a very young age, Hawking began his studies at Oxford University. His focus was on physics. The brilliant young man found his studies at the university to be quite easy.

01 June 1962

Hawking Receives First-Class Honors Degree

The end of his sojourn at Oxford University was evidence of young Hawking’s brilliance. He left Oxford with a first-class honors degree.

02 January 1963

Hawking is Diagnosed with ALS

Just before Hawking became 21 years old, he was diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS). He was told by medical professionals that he had only two years to live.

02 January 1965

Hawking Writes His Thesis

Hawking wrote his thesis based on an application of Roger Penrose’s theory of space-time singularity.
